Europe and its labour force

Below a map of Europe. The colors show the size of the labour force for each country in 2014, the darker the bigger. When you hover over the map you will see the exact amounts and when you click on a country it shows the labour force in more detail. Grey countries on the map unfortunately don't have any data available. Source: OECD.
The values on the x-axis:
em: Employment
em-a: Employment in Argiculture
em-f: Females in Employment
em-i: Employment in Industry
em-m: Males in Employment
em-s: Employment in Services
lf-f: Female Labourforce
lf-m: Male Labourforce
s-em: Self Employment
unem: Unemployment
